Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What's the relationship between Scitechia and THIA?

Scitechia Solutions is the technology services division of The Hope International Academy (THIA). Our projects are delivered by THIA's top-performing students under the direct supervision of our 40+ industry expert instructors. This unique model ensures quality while providing real-world experience to students.

How does student involvement affect quality?

Quality is never compromised. Every project is managed and reviewed at multiple stages by senior experts. Students work under close supervision, and final deliverables meet professional industry standards. Our experts have 5-15 years of industry experience at top Pakistani software houses.

What are your typical project timelines?

Timelines vary based on project complexity: Simple websites: 2-4 weeks | E-commerce: 6-10 weeks | Mobile apps: 8-16 weeks | Custom web applications: 8-20 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the discovery phase.

Do I own the complete source code?

Absolutely. You receive full ownership of all code, designs, and intellectual property developed for your project. There are no hidden licensing fees or restrictions.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, EasyPaisa, JazzCash, and online payments. Installment plans are available for larger projects. We offer flexible payment schedules to accommodate your budget.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, we're happy to sign non-disclosure agreements to protect your confidential information. Your project details and business ideas are safe with us.
