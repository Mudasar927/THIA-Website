Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about THIA

How do online classes work at THIA?

Our online classes are conducted via Zoom/Google Meet with interactive whiteboards, screen sharing, and real-time collaboration. Students receive recordings, study materials, and homework assignments through our learning management system.

What is the fee structure?

Fees vary by program, subject, and number of sessions per week. Click the calculator button on the right to use our interactive fee calculator!

Do you offer free trial classes?

Yes! We offer a free 30-minute trial session with one of our expert teachers. This helps you assess teaching quality and compatibility before committing to a full program.

Are your teachers qualified?

All our teachers hold advanced degrees (Masters/PhD) in their respective subjects with minimum 3+ years of teaching experience. Many have international teaching certifications and experience with various curricula.

Can I change my teacher if needed?

Absolutely! Student-teacher compatibility is crucial for effective learning. If you're not satisfied, we'll arrange a different teacher at no additional cost.
