Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about THIA

How do online classes work at THIA?

Our online classes are conducted via Zoom/Google Meet with interactive whiteboards, screen sharing, and real-time collaboration. Students receive recordings and study materials.

What is the fee structure?

Fees vary by program, subject, and number of sessions per week. Click the calculator button on the right to use our interactive fee calculator!

Do you offer free trial classes?

Yes! We offer a free 30-minute trial session with one of our expert teachers.

Are your teachers qualified?

All our teachers hold advanced degrees (Masters/PhD) in their respective subjects with minimum 3+ years of teaching experience.
